Our Products

Shrink Labels

With ever increasing competition in all kinds of commodity market, the importance of shelf impact and visual appearance is also increasing. Therefore, Rishabh Enterprises provides the industry with eye catching labels. The labels are excellent in design and print quality. They are easy to use and can be applied to a range of products.

Laminations

In food packaging applications, the product protection from oxygen and moisture and prevention from contamination is a critical issue. Therefore, we provide latest quality packaging solutions which not only preserve the product but also increments the brand equity of the product.

Adhesive Tapes

We provide an extensive range of BOPP self-adhesive tapes. These tapes are present in various colours and thickness. These adhesive tapes find various applications like packaging and carton sealing, stationary purposes, sealing purposes, advertising and marketing & steadying and bundling.
